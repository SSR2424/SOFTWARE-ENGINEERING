Do any limits apply to downloads?

No limits apply to downloads! Gone are the days of confusing credit plans or monthly limits – your one simple Envato Elements subscription allows you to download any asset you want as many times as you like.
However, per our User Terms and Fair Use Policy, please remember:

  1. The subscription is for an individual (or a specific number of people with our Team or Enterprise accounts).
  2. No robots allowed. So that means no sharing of accounts or use of automated tools to scrape and download items.
How does licensing work?

Our license is designed to make it easy for you to do the right thing and gives you the rights to use the item in a broad variety of projects, including those of a commercial nature.

Highlights of the Envato Elements subscription license:

  • All items available through our subscription have the same simple license terms.
  • Each time you download an item for a project, you get an ongoing license for a single, specified use. An item can be used again by registering it for another project.
  • Some common-sense limitations do apply. You can’t re-sell, re-distribute, use in on-demand services, or merchandise items without adding value.
  • Future proof licensing, even if you unsubscribe: If you unsubscribe, you can no longer use items from Envato for any new projects. However, any existing uses that you registered are still covered. Now that’s peace of mind!

Still have more questions about the License? Check the full list of FAQs from our support team.

Can I cancel or upgrade any time?

Absolutely! You can cancel or upgrade your Envato subscription at any time, with no cancellation fees.

Canceling
If you cancel your subscription, you will have access to Envato until the day your next subscription payment is due to be billed. For example, if you cancel your monthly subscription 23 days before the next payment, you will still have access for another 23 days. If you cancel your annual subscription three months before your payment is due, you will still get access for another three months. We do not refund canceled subscriptions.
However, as part of our worry-free licensing agreement, even after your subscription has ended you will be able to see the list of items you have previously licensed and continue using them for the projects they are licensed for. Learn more here.

Upgrading
If you wish to upgrade from a monthly to annual subscription for even more savings, the remaining days in your monthly subscription will be discounted from your annual subscription – so you don’t get charged twice. The total amount will be shown in your invoice.
